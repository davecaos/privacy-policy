4. Permissions We Request

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below)

Allows you to select meme images from your photo gallery. We only access files you explicitly select.

Modern permission for accessing photos. We respect all device restrictions.

We do NOT: Automatically scan all your photos, access any other files, or use this permission for anything other than selecting memes you want to store.
